Who I am

Mixed media artist with a focus on character design

I first discovered my love for art my freshman year of high school when I took a drawing class as an elective. That class opened my eyes to plethora of materials I could use to create my personal vision and the techniques of achieving realism. I continued taking art classes until I was given more freedom of the choice of subject and medium. Discovery of Photoshop and digital art was a game changer, and I gravitated to simplified 2D character illustrations.

In 2021, I began my education in computer engineering, but it didn't stifle my love of art. I continued drawing digitally and created some of my favorite pieces. In 2025 I returned to my traditional art roots. I also began to self study anatomy, which broadened the possibilities of my art subjects.

As improved my art and posted it on Instagram, I was encouraged to continue improving and sharing my work. to pursue my artistic path, and uncover worlds of wonders.

Now in 2026 I remain ever more adamant to pursue my artistic path and uncover worlds of wonders through physical expression presented in the form of visual art that conveys feelings and ideologies to all corners of the world without limits or discrimination.
